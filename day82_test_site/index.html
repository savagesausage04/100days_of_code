About Me.


Hi, my name is Kyle Han. I am a junior attending Beckman High School in Irvine, California. I will be part of the graduating class of 2024. In my free time, I like to play hangout with the cutest golden retiever, Kiwi. In the future, I intend on studying computer science.

Tennis has been a big part of my life for quite a while now. I started playing when I was 10, coming up on 7 years as a hobby. Whenever stress knocks at my door, tennis has always been my go-to option to clear my mind. I made the Beckman tennis team as a freshmen and intend to play through my entire high school journey.

Aside from school work, a large chunk of my time is devoted to coding. For majority of my life, I had no idea what I wanted to do. Humanities? Medicine? Law? I had no clue . . . until I discovered code. Coding has taught me so much about problem-solving. Can't wake up? Build your own alarm clock. The power of code is limitless. Coding has also led me to join the Beckman robotics team where we apply the same problem-solving aspects in a competition environment.
